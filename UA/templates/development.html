Development & Sustainability

Solar Energy Site Detection ➜ Urban Greening Opportunities ➜ Water Resource Map ➜

Our system introduces three additional sustainability-focused features. Solar Energy Site Detection uses map and image overlays to identify the most impactful sites for solar energy production, helping to build a more sustainable and energy-efficient city. Similarly, the Water Resource Map highlights rivers, lakes, and areas under water stress, enabling both better resource management and the detection of hydropower opportunities. Finally, Urban Greening Opportunities analyzes regions with low tree coverage and marks them for citizens and administrators, suggesting where planting trees could reduce risks such as heat, pollution, and environmental degradation. Together, these features enhance the city’s resilience and promote a greener, cleaner future.
