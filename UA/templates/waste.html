Waste Management

Waste Tracking ➜ Food Waste & Redistribution ➜

Our system will promote sustainability and waste reduction. Users can track waste through a form and dashboard, while a leaderboard highlights the most eco-friendly households or shops. Stores and bakeries can also log surplus food, and the system will suggest redistribution to nearby areas in need, reducing waste and improving community well-being.
