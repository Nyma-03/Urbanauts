Our Goals

Our website acts as a decision-support tool for urban planning by combining NASA Earth observation data with an interactive simulation. It benefits both planners and the general public in the following ways:

1. Data-Driven Insights
2. Visualization of Impact
3. Risk Awareness
4. Sustainable Development
5. User Engagement
6. Scenario Comparison

In short: Our website makes city planning more transparent, data-driven, and accessible. It not only supports professionals but also educates the public, leading to healthier, smarter, and more sustainable cities.
