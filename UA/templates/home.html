Urbanauts

Urbanauts is a web platform that uses NASA Earth observation data to guide smarter and greener city planning. It bridges the gap between science, technology, and communities to tackle challenges like air pollution, heatwaves, and loss of green spaces. By visualizing real-time data, it helps planners design sustainable solutions and empowers citizens to take part in building healthier environments.Urbanauts focuses on clean air, urban greening, and climate resilience to make cities livable for the future. It highlights the importance of balancing growth with nature to protect both people and ecosystems. The platform encourages awareness, participation, and innovation for sustainable living. With data-driven insights, it creates pathways for stronger communities and healthier urban spaces.Beyond monitoring and analysis, Urbanauts offers tools for planning public spaces, managing resources efficiently, and preparing for climate-related risks. It supports collaboration between local governments, urban planners, and residents, fostering a shared vision for sustainable development.By combining technology with community engagement, Urbanauts empowers citizens to contribute ideas, track progress, and adopt environmentally responsible behaviors.
