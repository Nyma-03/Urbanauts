Why NASA

NASA wants planners to ensure human quality of life remains high. Energy availability is part of that: cities with unreliable power affect health, water supply, and business productivity. By combining citizen reports with solar/renewable energy data, our website demonstrates smart strategies for city growth. NASA’s Earth science data is about climate, environment, disaster resilience, and urban planning.

Our app uses NASA satellite climate + hazard data (floods, heatwaves, risk zones). Addition of citizen science input (users reporting load shedding, waste issues, flooding, feedback and concerns). Then we turn it into action & learning with gamification (EcoCity Builder, Climate Challenges).

This way:
Users benefit (awareness + fun engagement + tracking their area).
Officials benefit (real-time input + better planning).
NASA benefits (their data is applied directly in community resilience + urban planning).
