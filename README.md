Urbanauts is a decision-support platform that combines **NASA Earth observation data**, **citizen science input**, and **gamification** to guide smarter, greener urban planning.  

Our goal is to empower **citizens, students, and planners** to make sustainable decisions that balance human wellbeing with environmental resilience.
